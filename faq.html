FAQ’s

Frequently Asked Questions
Q: What kind of data can you store?
A: With TAGS any data can be stored, searched and collected.
Q: How do I customize the information I want stored?
A: A representative from PDMI will work with you to identify the specific information you want to use and develop one or several modules that may be required to satisfy your data management needs.
Q: Who enters, deletes or finds the data in TAGS?
A: The choice is yours! The data can be accessed, changed, entered or deleted by you or others in your company that has permission granted user accounts. PDMI also offers round the clock technical support, data collection, data entry, and any other management needs you may have.
Q: How do I access my data?
A: After initial setup, you simply go to www.tagssoftware.com and enter your specific login information to begin.
A tutorial of these steps can be found on our TAGS page.
Q: Do I have to install certain software on my computer?
A: No, TAGS software is cloud-based and can be accessed with any web browser you choose. It can be accessed with any smartphone, tablet or computer, allowing you to make changes anywhere or anytime.
Q: Can I have more than one user with access to my data?
A: Of course! You can have as many users as you need. PDMI can also set up permissions for any user to allow viewing, editing, entering, and deleting your data.
Q: I have a large amount of data and sometimes I’m looking for one specific entry. Is there a way to search for what I’m looking for?
A: TAGS is equipped with a dynamic search feature that allows you to pin point exactly what you need, when you need it. A tutorial of these steps can be found on our TAGS page.
Q: Is TAGS difficult to use?
A: Not at all! With the fact that PDMI customizes the information to suit your needs everything you’re used to will be incorporated. Plus PDMI associates are always availalble to assist with any issues you may have.
Q: I am interested in using TAGS for my business, how do I get started?
A: Simply go to our contact us page to fill out our form and a PDMI representative will contact you.
