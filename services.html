Our Services

HomeOur Services
PDMI offers a complete range of data management solutions to its clients providing ease of integration with a simplified structure in retrieving specified information from any quantity of information stored.
The TAGS module system provided by PDMI is fully customizable to suit our clients unique needs.
PDMI has provided data management solutions for several different types of clients including, hospitals, facility management companies, municipalities, commercial and industrial companies.
Each of our clients use and store data differently allowing us to provide customized modules to satisfy any need our clients may have.
Some examples of modules created to facilitate our clients needs include:
  • Asbestos management
  • Lead management
  • Property management
  • Storage Tank management
  • Mechanical equipment management
  • Groundwater & Borehole monitoring
  • Noise monitoring
  • PCB monitoring
  • Mould monitoring
  • Waste management
  • Tenant Operation management
  • Elevator equipment management​​
  • Incident monitoring
​
With the power of TAGS any module can be created and customized to suit your needs
The options offered by PDMI to accommodate your data need are endless!
