Tracking Assets Group requests and Systems (TAGS)
TAGS is a secure data management software that is designed to allow users to easily enter, organize, retrieve and create reports from the data within their company either through user defined templates or ad hoc customized reports.
TAGS fully interacts with AdobeTM PDF and MicrosoftTM Excel, as most of our clients use on a daily basis.
TAGS has the unique capability of dynamic moduling that allows realtime changes for its clients. The customizable and adaptable design allows it to be used in a diverse range of industries.
TAGS is an easy to use, customizable and secure software that enables companies to fully utilize their data assets and retrieve it anywhere and anytime.
